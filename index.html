Secret Escape in Northern Thailand

The unseen part of northern Thailand is now ready for the you to enjoy. Surround yourself with nature and live like a local. Use us as a base to explore nearby attractions, or stop by on your way further north or south. Either way, you must come and see for yourself this amazing part of Thailand!

We have transformed our small farm into a cosy location with a local food cafe and a few rooms for travellers. Some time ago this land was used by our family for growing various plants and animals. We have cared about this land for decades and we are dedicated to make it even better. Out small "traveller's island" is owned and managed by our family, so by staying with us you support local people. Our kids have grown and went on to live in other parts of the world (one of them helped us to make this website!). We now enjoy staying on our land and taking care of our guests. We would be happy if you come to visit or stop by on your way between the north (Laos, China) and the south (central and couthern Thailand), or just come to visit Thung Chang and Nan for our local attractions. See you!
